What is SeedSphere?

SeedSphere augments magnet links with additional public trackers after validating their health. This helps peers find each other faster, improving start times and reducing stalls.

  • Health validation — Off, Basic (DNS + HTTP), or Aggressive with UDP handshakes.
  • Opt-in limits — Default unlimited healthy trackers; optionally cap how many to append.
  • Transparency — Recent Boosts panel shows what was actually applied.
