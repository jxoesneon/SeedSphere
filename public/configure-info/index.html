Configure your SeedSphere addon

Let's get your addon set up. If you arrived here from Stremio's Configure button, follow the steps below.

1. Sign in (website)

Make sure you are signed in on this origin. This lets us verify account ownership.

Open Start

2. Link your seedling

If this installation is new or unlinked, use Start to link it to your account.

Link Seedling

3. Check status

On the Account page you can view your seedlings and revoke or reinstall if needed.

Open Account
Why am I seeing this?
  • Unauthorized: You may not be signed in on this origin.
  • Revoked seedling: This installation was revoked. Mint or reinstall from the Account page.
  • Invalid link: The link signature is invalid or expired. Reopen Configure after signing in.
